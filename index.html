Our Manufacture

Revealing the
Soul of the Wood

Every piece of synagogue furniture we create is born from a deep respect for tradition and the natural beauty of wood. We do not mass-produce; instead, we treat each Aron Kodesh and Bimah as a unique masterpiece.

Our master carvers in Ukraine use time-honored techniques, blending intricate detailing with premium materials like solid oak and gold leaf. From the first sketch to the final polish, we ensure that our craftsmanship elevates the spiritual atmosphere of your sacred space.

Our Process

From Vision to Masterpiece

Creating sacred furniture is a journey. We work closely with your community at every step to ensure the final result exceeds expectations and perfectly fits your architectural space.

01

Bespoke Design & Engineering Blueprints

We start by understanding your synagogue's architecture and your community's needs. After this, we create meticulous technical plans, ensuring every piece integrates perfectly into your sacred space.

02

Wood Selection

We handpick premium solid wood, such as oak or mahogany, ensuring perfect grain and durability. The soul of the furniture begins with the right material.

03

Master Carving

Our skilled artisans bring the design to life. Using time-honored techniques, we meticulously carve every ornament, adding traditional motifs and custom gold leaf detailing.

04

Delivery & Installation

We don't just deliver; we carefully install the furniture in your synagogue, ensuring perfect alignment, structural integrity, and a flawless final finish.
