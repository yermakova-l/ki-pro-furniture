YouTube: Our Website includes embedded YouTube videos showing completed projects and manufacturing processes. These videos are not loaded automatically when you first visit the Website. They are loaded only when you choose to play a video or allow external media cookies. We use YouTube’s privacy-enhanced mode via youtube-nocookie.com. When you play an embedded YouTube video, YouTube may set cookies or process information about your device, browser, IP address and interaction with the video according to its own privacy and cookie policies.

Analytics Cookies: Tools like Google Analytics help us understand how visitors use our site, allowing us to improve its design and functionality.

CookieScript: We use this tool to manage your consent preferences in compliance with UK law.

How to manage cookies: You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. You can also manage your preferences through the Cookie Consent Banner on our Website.
